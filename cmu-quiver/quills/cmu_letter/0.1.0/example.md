On behalf of Dietrich College of Humanities and Social Sciences, thank you for the Whitfield Family Foundation's generous gift in support of the Dietrich Analysis Research Education (DARE) platform. Your investment arrives at a pivotal moment for our students and faculty.

Over the past academic year, DARE has given more than 2,000 students vendor-agnostic access to frontier AI models, file-grounded research tools, and reproducible workflows — capabilities that were previously out of reach for courses without dedicated technical staff. Your gift will extend this access to first-year writing and public policy courses across the college.

Enclosed, please find the pilot-year impact report and the stewardship schedule for the coming year. We would be delighted to host you on campus this fall to see the platform in use and to introduce you to the students whose work it supports.

Thank you again for your partnership and your confidence in our mission.
